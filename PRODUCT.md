## What is StrideShip?

StrideShip builds **custom operational systems for logistics businesses**. Not software products. Not SaaS. Not dashboards you log into and configure yourself.

We are a **bespoke systems firm** — we study how a logistics operation actually runs, identify every manual touchpoint that is eating time and money, and replace it with precision-engineered automation. End-to-end. Custom. No templates.

The core promise: **automate the manual infrastructure of global trade.**

---

## Who Is This For?

**Primary buyer:** The operational decision-maker inside a mid-size Indian logistics firm. Think COO, Head of Operations, or the founder who is still personally managing ops. They are smart, stretched thin, and deeply skeptical of generic software vendors.

**Specific client verticals:**
- LCL Consolidators
- 3PL Providers
- Freight Forwarders
- NVOCC Operators
- Custom House Agents (CHAs)
- Clearance Agents
- Importers & Exporters
- Logistics Firms (general)

**Their daily reality:**
- Teams losing 6–8 hours a day on coordination that should be automated
- ICEGATE query loops eating weekends
- Customs documents manually re-keyed across 3–6 systems per shipment
- Demurrage charges from documentation errors that a system would have caught
- Shipment status only known when someone physically checks
- Reports built in spreadsheets the night before a leadership review
- ₹50,000+ lost per container from a single missed customs query at Nhava Sheva

**What they are NOT:**
- They are not early-stage startups experimenting with automation
- They are not enterprise conglomerates with dedicated IT teams
- They are not interested in yet another SaaS dashboard they have to manage
- They have been burned by off-the-shelf tools that don't fit their workflows

## What We Actually Build

1. **Workflow Automation** — Replace end-to-end manual processes with intelligent systems. RFQ pipelines, shipment coordination, customs document loops.
2. **Document Processing** — Parse PDFs, invoices, packing lists, and quote emails into structured outputs instantly, with zero manual copy-paste.
3. **RFQ & Quote Automation** — Turn unstructured email requests into accurate, formatted quotes in seconds, with a full audit trail.
4. **Shipment Tracking Systems** — Multi-carrier real-time visibility in one place, with exception-based alerts before clients notice delays.
5. **Operational Dashboards** — Live KPIs, team performance, pipeline status. Leadership gets answers without asking anyone.
6. **System Integration** — Connect CRM, ERP, carrier APIs, and internal tools into a unified operational layer.

Everything is **custom-built to each client's workflows, data structure, and scale**. No templates. No shortcuts.

We work with a **limited number of clients**. Quality over volume. Always.

## Visual Design System

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#060B14` — near-black, deep navy. Not pure black.
- Primary text: `#F1F5F9` — off-white slate.
- Secondary text: `#94A3B8`, `#CBD5E1`, `#64748B` — layered greys.
- Accent: `#6B8FA8` — muted steel blue. Used sparingly: glows, borders, highlights, bullet dots.
- Glass surfaces: `rgba(10, 15, 30, 0.75)` with `inset 0 1px 0 rgba(255,255,255,0.06)` highlight.

**Typography:**
- Headings: Work Sans — tight tracking (`-0.03em` to `-0.04em`), high weight (700–800), short line-height (~0.95).
- Italic/contrast text: Libre Baskerville — used for subordinate headline clauses, muted slate color.
- Data/numbers: Space Grotesk — tabular weight for stat displays.

**Design language:**
- Heavy glassmorphism: dark translucent surfaces with inset highlight lines, no hard borders.
- Abstract SVG geometry as decorative background elements (matte gradient fills, heavy drop shadows, low opacity ~0.35).
- Motion: Framer Motion with `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` ease — fast in, slow settle.
- Scale: Large, architectural typography. Headlines at `clamp(2.5rem, 6vw, 4.5rem)` to `clamp(3.5rem, 7vw, 6rem)`.
- Layout: `width: 80%`, centered, with fluid clamp padding. Not a grid framework.

**Interactive behavior:**
- Hover reveals content (expandable cards) — not click-only.
- Accent color bleeds in on hover: border changes, icon color shifts, glow appears.
- Scroll-driven sections (sticky positioning, progress-based content swaps).
